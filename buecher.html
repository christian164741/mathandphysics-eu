Photon – Theory and Applications

From Classical Physics to Quantum Mechanics

What is a photon – wave, particle, or both? This book takes you from the classical concept of light to quantum mechanics and shows why photons shape modern technology and fundamental research. Clear, precise, and well-founded.

For interested laypeople, students, and everyone who truly wants to understand physics.

Highlights:

  • Compact overview from classical physics to quantum mechanics
  • Clear explanations without prior knowledge of advanced mathematics
  • Didactic boxes and examples make access easier
  • Suitable for beginners as well as for students seeking deeper insights

Available in German and English

This book was created with the support of modern AI tools (e.g. ChatGPT) for phrasing, structuring, and smoothing the texts. Full content responsibility and scientific review remain entirely with the author.
